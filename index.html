OUR GAMES:

About our games

As a small garage indie group, we have limited resources in what we put into our games. With that said, we work tirelessly and we hope that you enjoy our upcoming title Kingdom Forge and the others to come in the (hopefully) near future.

Kingdom Forge

Kingdom Forge is an upcoming 2D RPG that was inspired by old RPG classics such as Final Fantasy built with the most modern of 8-bit graphics. The game is set to follow three major storylines all their own quests but at the same time, all interleaving into one grand quest for fame, glory, revenge, and maybe a little bit of fun.
